Popular questions

Go to "My Orders" in your dashboard and select the order you'd like to track. You'll see its current status — Processing, Shipped, or Delivered — along with an estimated delivery date.

We accept mobile money (Airtel Money, MTN Mobile Money, Zamtel Kwacha), major debit/credit cards, and cash on delivery in select areas.

Open the order in "My Orders" and select "Request return". Most items are eligible for return within 7 days of delivery, provided they're unused and in original packaging.

Register for an account and choose "Seller" as your account type. You'll be asked for a few verification details before you can start listing products.

On the login page, select "Forgot password?" and follow the instructions sent to your registered email or phone number.
