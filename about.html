Our story

Started in Lusaka, built for the whole country.

Zambian Marketplace began as a small directory for local sellers who had great products but nowhere reliable to list them. We saw buyers struggling to find trustworthy sellers outside their neighbourhood, and sellers struggling to be found beyond it.

Today, the platform connects tailors, farmers, electronics traders, and thousands of other small businesses directly with customers — with local payment options, direct messaging, and delivery partners built around how people actually shop here.
